Waffle recipe

In their simplest form (wafer), waffles are a flat pastry made from flour and water and baked between two hot irons (waffle irons).

There are many variations based on the type of waffle iron and recipe used. Waffles are eaten throughout the world, particularly in Belgium, which has over a dozen regional varieties. Waffles may be made fresh or simply heated after having been commercially cooked and frozen.

    Waffle ingredients

  • 125 gramm Soft butter
  • 100 gramm Sugar
  • 1 packet Vanille sugar
  • 3 Eggs
  • 250 gramm Flour
  • 1 pinch of Salt
  • 1 teaspoon Baking powder
  • 200ml Milk

    Steps

  1. Mix butter with sugar and vanilla sugar. Gradually stir in eggs.
  2. Mix the flour, baking powder and salt together and stir into the dough.
  3. Now slowly stir in the milk.
  4. Preheat the waffle iron and place 2 heaped tablespoons of batter onto each waffle iron.
  5. Cook each waffle until golden brown, about 1-2 minutes. Enjoy with fresh fruit or cream.
