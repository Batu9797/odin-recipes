Omelette recipe

An omelet or omelette is an egg dish characterized by an elongated oval shape with a smooth surface. The inside typically has a juicy, soft consistency and there should be no browning on the outside.

    Omelette ingredients

  • 6 Eggs
  • 1 pinch of Salt
  • 1 pinch of Pepper from the mill
  • 1 tablespoon Butter for the pan
  • 6 tablespoons Milk
  1. To make the omelette, put the eggs and a little milk in a bowl, season with salt and pepper and whisk well.
  2. Then melt the butter in a pan and pour the egg mixture into the pan with a ladle - swirling the pan to distribute the egg mixture evenly in the pan - and let it set (do not turn it).
  3. Finally, fold one half towards the middle with a spatula.
  4. Place the omelette on a plate and serve garnished with finely chopped herbs and/or tomato pieces
