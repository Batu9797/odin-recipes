Sucuk with eggs recipe

Sucuk is often served for breakfast in Turkey - it is a spiced beef sausage seasoned with garlic.

Common popular variations of sucuk with egg include the addition of fried pepper strips, tomato pieces or onion rings. And if you like it spicier you can also add a little Pul Biber (=chilli flakes).

    Sucuk with eggs ingredients

  • 0.5 pieces Sucuk, Turkish garlic sausage made from beef
  • 4 Eggs
  • 1 pinch of Salt
  • 1 pinch of Pepper
  • 1 spoon Oil or butter into the pan

    Steps

  1. Remove the skin from the sucuk sausage and cut into 5 mm thick slices.
  2. Then put the sucuk slices in a large pan with a little butter and sauté them lightly on both sides
  3. Nun die Eier als Spiegelei in die Pfanne geben und das Spiegelei bis zur gewünschten Konsistenz fertig braten.
  4. Das türkische Gericht zum Schluss mit Salz und Pfeffer und eventuell klein gezupfter Petersilie würzen und sofort servieren.
